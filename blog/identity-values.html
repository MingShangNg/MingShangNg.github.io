Introduction

My name is Ming Shang Ng. I was born in Malaysia and moved to New Zealand when I was sixteen. I finished my high school here but dropped out of university because I didn’t like university. I have been working at supermarkets for at least six years (part time and full time). I changed my career path from supermarket to hospitality (cooking and baking) because I can’t see any more opportunities for me in the supermarket.I have been working in hospitality for four years and I really like it until covid pandemic happens. During the covid pandemic, the hospitality industry got hit pretty badly. This pandemic really made me anxious and think about my future career path in hospitality. Fortunately, I have great support from my family during the pandemic. My brother advises me to change my career path to the tech industry since I spend a lot of time on my computer and so I follow it.

Personal Value & Culture

I grew up with a family of five (youngest in the family) in Malaysia. When I was young, I was exposed to many different kinds of culture because Malaysia is a multicultural country.Our family has strong family values. My parents always teach us to work hard and take care of our family members, especially my mum. My mum is one of the biggest influences in my life. She will always “discipline” me when I was young and teach me to work hard and to be strong.

Strength (Hard working, enthusiastic and supportive)

My strengths have allowed me to achieve some of my goals in my life. For example, while working in the supermarket, I was very hard working and enthusiastic to learn how to do the order and negotiate with the supplier. I was also teaching and sharing new knowledge to my new and old colleagues.

Weakness (Shy, Repress my feeling and overload myself)

My weaknesses have given me problems in my life. For example, while working in the supermarket, sometimes I overwork myself because I didn’t express my thoughts to my colleagues and managers. I am bad at expressing my feelings to other people because maybe when I was young, my mum always taught me to be strong and show no emotion. Currently, I am trying to understand and improve myself by reading more books and trying to express more my feelings to other people.

Ethical decision, experience resistance or tension from the experiences

In my previous work experiences, working as a team is very important. I remember when I was a supervisor at a supermarket running the promotion team. One of my member not working well with my team and sometimes he just decides to not show up for work so I have to make a decision to remove him from my team because it is not fair to the other members. This decision has given my team a better work environment but it did cause friction between me and him.
