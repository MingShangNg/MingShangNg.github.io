Neuroplasticity

Back in the day, scientists thought our brain was static and unchanged but recent studies show that this is false. In fact our brain is constantly changing and adaptable. This is called Neuroplasticity

How understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people? Neuroplasticity refers to the brainâ€™s ability to reorganize itself by forming new neural connections throughout life. Neuroplasticity is a lifelong phenomenon, which means our brain is constantly changing at any time with repeated and specialized training/ instruction even an injured brain can change and recover. The brain can change itself in three different ways:

  1. Chemical. The brain functions by transferring chemical signals between brain cells which are neurons and this triggers the action and reaction. Each time the brain learns new things, the brain can increase the concentration of chemical signals for neurons. Because this way can change rapidly so it is only good for short term memory.
  2. Brain Structure. Each time the brain learns new things, the brain will start changing the structure between the neurons. Changing the structure of the brain takes a long time so it means this type of change is for long term memory.
  3. Alternate function in part of the brain. Each time the brain learns new things, the different parts of the brain will start to activate.

The benefits of neuroplasticity to people are ability to learn new things, enhance existing cognitive capabilities, recovery from brain injuries, strengthening areas if some functions are lost or decline and improvements that can boost brain fitness.

How might you engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your own benefit?

We can engage the principles of neuroplasticity for our own benefit by focusing our behavior

  1. Having a positive mind.
  2. Identify good habits and bad habits.
  3. Challenging our brains and practicing more.

Growth Mindset

Growth mindset is the mindset for individuals who believe their work and talents can develop through hard work, work plan and with help from others. Having a growth mindset is important because it can help individuals to overcome obstacles when learning or developing new skills. During this exploration, I found out the importance of having a growth mindset. It can give me more freedom and learn more new skills. Having a growth mindset is very important with my learning journey because it can help me become more comfortable facing new challenges and accepting having mistakes is a good opportunity to learn and grow.
